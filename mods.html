Mods

Personally I'd call this more of a place for my projects but it's fine

ResourceFix

Currently discontinued at the moment, I might work on it again soon.

ResourceFix is a resource pack that reroutes 1.12.2 models to use their 1.13 equivalents.

This allows most 1.13+ texture packs to run on 1.12.2.

1.8 is currently broken due to it using different blockstate syntax.


The 128MB Pack

A simple modpack for people who really want to push their game to the bare minimum.

Note: Crashes extremely frequently so be ready for that

MCUnlocked

One of my largest projects, MCUnlocked currently spans through every single version to a1.0.15 - 1.7.10.


Note: b1.5_01, b1.4_01, and a1.2.0 - 1.2.3 are left out due to MCP not supporting them entirely, or properly.

BedCraft

Currently in progress, BedCraft is a mod that breaks 1.5.2 at high distances as thoroughly as possible.


Development report: As of 11/29 the mod is pretty much finished, but the Far Lands are being worked on.

XRay+

XRay+ is a simple and lightweight XRay texure pack that focuses on being lightweight and minimalistic.

I originally started it due to my MacBook being unable to run it stably, but now it serves as a fast XRay alternative for low end computers.
